---
title: PX4
---

Foxglove can load and inspect [PX4 ULog](https://docs.px4.io/main/en/dev_log/ulog_file_format.html) (`.ulg`) files stored locally on your computer.

To load a local PX4 ULog (`.ulg`) file, double-click it from your file manager, drag-and-drop the file directly into the app, or _Open local file_ via the _Data source_ dialog:

![local file dialog](/img/docs/studio/connection/local-file/dialog.webp)
